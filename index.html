To the Light of My Life

My dearest, every beat of my heart echoes your name. In the silence of dawn and the hush of dusk, your presence lives within me, soft and eternal.

Loving you is like breathing effortless and essential. I count stars not to dream, but to remember every moment with you.

No poem, no verse, no symphony could hold the weight of my affection. You are my muse, my melody, my morning light.
